Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

CreditKarma.in ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at creditkarma.in. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect when you visit our site, how we use it, whom we share it with (if anyone), and what choices you have. We wrote this document in plain language so you can understand it without a law degree — but it is still a legal policy, so read it carefully.

Important: We are an independent information website. We are not Credit Karma (creditkarma.com), Intuit, or a credit bureau. This policy applies only to creditkarma.in.

Information we collect

Information you voluntarily provide

If you use our contact form, we may collect your name, email address, subject line, and message content. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry, process corrections, or consider topic suggestions. We do not require account registration to read our articles.

Automatically collected technical data

Like most websites, our servers and analytics tools may automatically record certain technical information when you visit:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address (often truncated or anonymized in analytics)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and device type
  • Pages viewed, time spent, and navigation path
  • Referring website or search query (when available)
  • Date and time of access

This data helps us understand which articles are useful, fix broken links, detect abuse, and improve site performance. It is not used to pull your credit report or make lending decisions — we do not have access to your credit file.

Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We may use:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies — to measure traffic and usage patterns in aggregate
  • Advertising cookies — to show, limit, measure, and improve ads if advertising is enabled on the site
  • Preference cookies — to remember choices you make (where applicable)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking all cookies may affect how some features work. We do not use cookies to sell your personal information.

How we use your information

We use collected information for legitimate purposes related to operating an educational website:

  • Responding to contact form submissions and reader feedback
  • Improving article quality, site design, and navigation
  • Monitoring security, preventing spam, and enforcing our terms
  • Complying with applicable laws, regulations, or legal process
  • Producing aggregated, non-identifying statistics about readership trends

We do not use your data to offer loans, credit cards, or insurance. We do not sell your email address to marketing lists.

Legal bases for processing (where applicable)

Depending on your location, privacy laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) or state privacy statutes may apply. Where required, we process personal information based on:

  • Your consent (for example, when you submit the contact form)
  • Our legitimate interest in operating and improving the website
  • Compliance with legal obligations

Third-party services

We may rely on trusted service providers to host the website, deliver email, or provide analytics. These providers process data on our behalf under contractual obligations to protect it and use it only for the services they perform for us. Each provider maintains its own privacy policy governing its practices.

Our articles may contain links to external sites (government agencies, bureaus, news sources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. Review their policies before submitting personal information.

Advertising and Google services

CreditKarma.in may display ads served by Google or other advertising partners. Advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to help show relevant ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and limit how often the same ad appears.

Google may use advertising cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites. You can manage Google ad personalization through Google Ad Settings. You can also learn more about how Google uses data from partner sites at Google's partner sites policy.

If personalized ads are not appropriate or available, ads may still appear based on the page content, general location, browser type, or other non-personalized signals. We do not ask visitors to create an account, and we do not provide credit reports, lending decisions, or financial products directly.

Data retention

We retain contact form messages for as long as needed to address your inquiry and maintain reasonable business records, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Server logs are typically rotated or deleted on a schedule appropriate to security and analytics needs.

Data security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No website or internet transmission is completely secure. Use our contact form only for non-sensitive communications — never send Social Security numbers, full account numbers, or passwords.

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of certain information
  • Opt out of certain analytics or marketing (we do not sell personal information)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

To submit a request, use our contact page with the subject "Privacy Request." We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

Our site is intended for a general audience age 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in certain jurisdictions). If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
