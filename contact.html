What we cannot do

CreditKarma.in is an information site, not a financial services company. We cannot:

  • Pull or change your credit report or score
  • Approve, deny, or influence loan applications
  • Provide personalized financial, legal, or tax advice by email
  • Access or troubleshoot your Credit Karma (creditkarma.com) account
  • Resolve disputes between you and a lender, bureau, or collector

For account issues with Credit Karma the company, contact their official support channels at creditkarma.com. For credit report disputes, file directly with AnnualCreditReport.com or the bureaus.
